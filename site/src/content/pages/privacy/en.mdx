**Effective date**: May 7, 2026

## 1. Who we are

Vertex is a privacy-focused VPN service operated by an independent team. Contact: [support@vertices.ru](mailto:support@vertices.ru).

## 2. The bottom line

> **We can't see your traffic. The relay vertex is mathematically blind.**

Vertex uses end-to-end encryption (X25519 Diffie-Hellman + ChaCha20-Poly1305) between your device and the exit server. The relay vertex that forwards your packets — even one we operate ourselves — has no key material capable of decrypting them. It can only observe TCP-level metadata of the connection itself (source IP, packet timestamps, packet sizes), never the content.

## 3. What data we collect

To run the service, we process the minimum necessary:

- **Account credentials**: a username (e.g., `vtx-client-yourname`) and a salted, hashed password (bcrypt) stored on the relay vertex for authentication.
- **Device identity public key**: a 32-byte X25519 public key your client generates on first launch. Used for our anti-credential-sharing protection (TOFU pinning). The corresponding private key never leaves your device.
- **Connection metadata**: ephemeral relay logs containing connection timestamps and source IP addresses, retained for up to 7 days for abuse prevention. Not linked to user identity beyond username.

## 4. What we do NOT collect

We do not store, log, or analyze:

- Your traffic content (we cannot — see Section 2)
- DNS queries
- Browsing history or visited domains
- Persistent device identifiers, advertising IDs
- Location data, contacts, photos, microphone, or any device sensor data
- Telemetry, analytics, crash reports (unless you opt-in via your operating system)

## 5. Third parties

Vertex runs on multiple independent infrastructure providers across several regions (sub-processors). They host our relay vertices and exit servers but see only encrypted traffic; they cannot read packet contents. The current list of providers and regions is available on request — email [support@vertices.ru](mailto:support@vertices.ru).

We do not share user data with advertisers, analytics services, or any third party.

## 6. Data retention

- **Relay logs**: up to 7 days, then automatically rotated and destroyed.
- **Account credentials**: retained while your account is active. Deleted on request.
- **Device identity public keys**: retained on exit servers until you request deletion or your account is removed.

## 7. Your rights

Regardless of your jurisdiction, you have the following rights:

- **Right to deletion** — email [support@vertices.ru](mailto:support@vertices.ru) with your username; we will remove your credentials and identity key entries within 30 days.
- **Right to access** — request a copy of all data we hold about you (which is limited: username, identity public key hex, last-seen timestamp).
- **Right to portability** — your identity key is stored on your device; you can export it yourself (see Support page).

## 8. Children

Vertex is not directed at users under 13 (or under 16 in jurisdictions with stricter rules under GDPR). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will publish the new version on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be announced via the Support page.
